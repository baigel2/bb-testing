To make a boy a man, get up and go on forth.

Do what you know needs doing, if it's easy or if its hard.

Steady drop defeats fleeting torrent, as the pen defeats the sword.

Persistence, discipline, humility. Consistency is key.

The empty mind reveals a mirror,

To the eddies of your soul.
