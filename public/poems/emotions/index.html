Emotions

What are we without them?

And with them, volcanoes, earthquakes, tsunamis and fire.

Time and again, in my heart, a desire,

to be Vulcan but alas I’m human and no higher.

But if I am to accept, I’m no more than a human,

then it must also be true, that I am,

no less.
